Air Quality Analysis using Purple Air

The testbed has a variety of Air Quality sensors created by Purple Air. These sensors report back a variety of information including temperature, humidity, particle matter content and more. Below is a realtime demonstration of the PurpleAir readings throughout the testbed. These readings come from a server which grabs the data from our Kafka servers, and publishes it in a way that can be accessed anywhere.
